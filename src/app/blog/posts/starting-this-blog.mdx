I'm Abubakir, 16, from Kokand, Uzbekistan. I'm teaching myself Python and the foundations of AI in my free time, and I decided to keep this blog so I have somewhere to think out loud.

The plan is simple. I follow a roadmap I keep for myself, work through one thing at a time, and write a short note whenever something finally clicks. I'd rather understand a concept properly than rush past it, so most posts here will be small: a problem I got stuck on, what the fix turned out to be, and what I'd tell myself before starting.

I use modern AI tools every day to learn faster — Claude, DeepSeek, Gemini, and Hugging Face — and I'm getting comfortable with the everyday developer stack along the way: Git, Linux, Docker, and the major cloud platforms. None of it is finished. That's sort of the point of writing it down.

If you want to follow along, I'm most active on Telegram. Thanks for reading.
